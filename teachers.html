Meet Our Teachers

Roché Apinsa

Roché is a passionate lock teacher with over 20 years of experience. He has a unique teaching style that combines technical precision with creative expression, inspiring students to find their own voice in locking.

Ruben 'Chi' Verhoeven

Ruben 'Chi' Verhoeven is a dedicated lock teacher known for his dynamic classes and ability to break down complex moves into easy-to-understand steps. He emphasizes musicality and encourages students to express themselves through locking.

'Fresh' Seiji

'Fresh' Seiji is a lock teacher with a vibrant personality and a deep love for the culture of locking. He brings energy and enthusiasm to his classes, creating a fun and supportive learning environment for all students.
